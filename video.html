The Crystal Charter


A YA+ Audiobook


Maebe Sumine is the sole heir to a long line of wealthy magic merchants who serve the ruler of Grean.

The Sumine’s are permitted to trade with a witch ruled countries in the south for items allowed under the strict guidelines of the Crystal Charter. In exchange for the lucrative contracts, her family is bound to make an annual gesture of loyalty by transporting the King’s Burden.

Maebe’s dedication to her family’s duty is strained when her father marries a woman only two years older than her. She abandons her position as a trader to visit the enchanting city of Clest, home of the Guild of Artificer Witches. There, she begins to unravel secrets from her countries war stained past. Loyalty is a heavy burden, and not always worth the price.

Is Maebe’s fate sealed in red?
