About Me

I can say that I enjoy writing but what I love is bringing worlds to life.
Drawing pictures with my words in other peoples minds, like a child with a new box of felt tip pens and giant sheet of paper. Whether it be through a full length novel, an audiobook, blog or a short story--as long as I'm creating, I'm happy.
